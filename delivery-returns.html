Delivery & Returns

We deliver seven days a week. Same-day delivery applies to orders confirmed before 4pm within Lagos. Next-day delivery applies nationwide.

Delivery fees

  • Lagos Mainland: ₦1,200 (free above ₦25,000)
  • Lagos Island & Lekki-Ajah: ₦1,500
  • Nationwide: Calculated at checkout based on postcode

Returns policy

Not satisfied with an item? Report within 24 hours for replacement or refund. Perishables must remain sealed or chilled for collection.
