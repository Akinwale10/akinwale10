Delivery options

  • Same-day: Order by 4pm for delivery within 90 minutes across Owode Yewa and Ilaro Road.
  • Evening runs: Catch our 6pm – 8pm rider wave covering Idi Iroko and neighbouring border communities.
  • Click & collect: Skip the queue with curbside pickup at Isaga Bus Stop, opposite The Gem International School.
  • Scheduled plans: Set weekly or monthly grocery drops for homes, schools, and restaurants.

Returns & replacements

We operate a 24-hour freshness guarantee. If an item arrives damaged, missing, or not quite right, we’ll replace or refund instantly.

  1. Report within 24 hours: Use the order timeline or WhatsApp us photos/videos.
  2. Choose resolution: Refund to original payment, loyalty points, or replacement on next delivery.
  3. Collection: Keep items chilled or sealed. Our rider will pick them up during your next slot.

For health reasons, frozen foods and dairy must remain sealed for us to process returns.
