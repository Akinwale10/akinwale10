About OMOOLA SUPERMARKET

OMOOLA SUPERMARKET is a proudly Nigerian grocery experience connecting families to fresh produce, pantry staples, and home essentials at transparent prices. From our Lagos hub, we partner with local farmers, trusted importers, and community riders to deliver groceries on the same day.

Our promise

  • Freshness guaranteed: produce harvested within 24 hours and proteins kept at optimal temperatures.
  • Community-first: we support smallholder farmers and empower delivery partners with fair wages.
  • Digital convenience: easy search, wishlist, loyalty points, and instant WhatsApp support.

Sustainability

We use reusable crates, optimise delivery routes, and champion responsible sourcing. Every order supports food rescue programmes in Lagos Mainland.
