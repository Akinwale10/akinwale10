    About OMOOLA SUPERMARKET STORES

    Rooted in hospitality. Elevated for modern living.

    Born in Owode Yewa as a family-run provision shop, OMOOLA SUPERMARKET STORES now delivers a cosmopolitan retail experience for households, restaurants, and corporate clients. We blend smart inventory systems with neighbourly warmth so your everyday essentials are always in-stock, beautifully displayed, and served with genuine care.
