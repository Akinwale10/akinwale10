    About OMOOLA SUPERMARKET STORES

    Rooted in hospitality. Driven by innovation.

    OMOOLA SUPERMARKET STORES began as a family-led neighborhood provision store and has grown into a trusted retail destination serving Owode Yewa, Ogun State. We blend warm, human service with tech-enabled inventory, ensuring every aisle is filled with essentials your household depends on.
